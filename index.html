APD Crime Visualization

Hover your cursor over the cells to see a count of reported criminal incidents in Austin, TX for that particular date.

Color Scale:       

The sequential color scale renders progressively darker colors when there is a higher frequency of incidents on a given day.

If there are no incidents reported for a particular day or there is no data available yet, then the square is left blank.

The diverging color scale renders progressively darker colors when the count of incidents for a given day falls within one, two, or three standard deviations from the mean.

Shades of green represent fewer incidents than average, whereas shades of red represent the opposite.


Sort List By:       

You can select the type of crime from the drop-down list above and see the distribution of reported incidents for that particular category.

Sorting the drop-down list by frequency will make it easier to find interesting categories to examine. The option to view all incidents will always be at the top.
